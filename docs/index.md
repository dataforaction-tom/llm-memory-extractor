# LLM Memory Extractor

A browser extension that watches your conversations with AI chatbots and quietly builds a personal memory profile — so you don't have to repeat yourself every time you start a new chat.

## What it does

When you chat with an AI assistant, you share all sorts of things about yourself — your job, your preferences, your goals. But those details vanish when the conversation ends. LLM Memory Extractor captures that information automatically and organises it into a personal knowledge base you own and control.

It works silently in the background across six major AI platforms, extracts personal facts using an LLM of your choice (including fully local options), and lets you review everything before it's saved.

## Getting started

1. **Install the extension** in Chrome or Firefox
2. **Visit any supported AI chat** — Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Mistral Chat, or Grok
3. **Start a conversation** — you'll see a small capture button appear on the page
4. **Click the capture button** to record the conversation
5. **Open the side panel** by clicking the extension icon — your extracted facts will appear on the Dashboard

From there, you can review facts, organise them into documents, and export your memory profile however you like.
